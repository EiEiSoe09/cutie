✨ Luxury Matcha Experience ✨

Step into a world where the aroma of fresh, dewy matcha and delicate sweetness envelops you. At Starbucks® Matcha Shop, we offer a truly luxurious matcha experience – enjoy velvety, sweet lattes, fluffy pancakes, serene matcha ice cream, refreshing strawberry matcha frappes, and a variety of other delectable treats.

Additionally, we feature Japanese-style desserts such as Dorayaki, Matcha Roll Cake, and Anmitsu, allowing you to indulge in soft, sweet, and delicate Japanese delights.

Each cup is crafted with high-quality matcha powder, combining fresh, natural green color with a signature flavor. Whether you’re a matcha lover or a first-time taster, our menu promises to amaze your taste buds.
