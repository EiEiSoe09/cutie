Matcha Cheesecake

A premium dessert that perfectly combines the rich, creamy, and slightly bitter taste of matcha with the smooth, velvety texture of cheesecake. The sweet and earthy flavor balances well, making it perfect for special occasions.

👩‍🍳 Instructions

1. Mix biscuit crumbs and melted butter and press into cake pan
2. Blend cream cheese and sugar until smooth
3. Add eggs one at a time and blend
4. Add whipping cream, matcha powder and vanilla extract and mix until smooth
5. Pour mixture over crust and smooth out
6. Bake in 160°C oven for 50–60 minutes
7. After baking, let cool and chill in fridge for 3–4 hours
8. Slice and serve 🍰
