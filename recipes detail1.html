Matcha Latte

A classic Japanese-style drink that's creamy, smooth, and balances the slightly bitter taste of matcha with the smoothness of milk.

👩‍🍳 Instructions

1. Sift the matcha powder into a bowl
2. Add hot water and whisk until smooth
3. Prepare the milk, either hot or cold
4. Add the milk to the matcha mixture and mix well
5. Add sweetener to taste
6. Add whipped cream and sprinkle a little matcha powder
