Matcha Cake

A classic dessert with a soft and fluffy cake texture and the slightly bitter taste of matcha balanced with sweet creaminess. Perfect for tea lovers.

👩‍🍳 Instructions

1.Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F)
2. Combine flour, matcha powder and baking powder in a bowl
3. Whisk eggs and sugar until fluffy
4. Add milk and melted butter and mix
5. Slowly add dry ingredients to make a smooth batter
6. Pour into baking pan and bake for 40–45 mins
7. When cake is cool, you can decorate with cream
