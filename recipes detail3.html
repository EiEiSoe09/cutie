Matcha Ice Cream

Creamy, smooth, and perfectly balanced with the slightly bitter taste of matcha, this refreshing dessert is perfect for the hot summer months and a must-try for matcha lovers.

👩‍🍳 Instructions

1. Add the matcha powder to a bowl and mix with the milk until smooth
2. Whisk the whipping cream until fluffy
3. Add the condensed milk and fold gently
4. Add the matcha mixture and mix evenly
5. Put the mixture in a container and freeze for 6–8 hours
6. Scoop and serve 🍦
